I'm a 1st-year Ph.D. student at University of Warwick, UK, advised by Dr. Abhir Bhalerao and Prof. Yulan He. My research interests lie in the field of Computer Vision and Image Processing, particularly in hige-level image understanding and fine-grained visual categorization. I'm also affiliated with the Integrated and Intelligent Systems, Griffith University, Australia supervised by Prof. Yongsheng Gao and Dr. Xiaohan Yu. I collaborated with them on a series of works.

I received my M.Sc degree in Artificial Intelligence with the best overall performance from King's College London (KCL) , London, UK.
